About GMU ACM

The GMU ACM is a student chapter of the oldest computing organization in the world, the Association for Computing Machinery (External Link). Our goals are to provide a forum for students interested in computers, provide networking opportunities for students with other bright ACM members and technology organizations, and provide its members the benefits of exposure to the state-of-the-art developments in computing technology.

Activities

- Training for the ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest (External Link). Yearly regionals are held in various parts of the world.

- We also provide publicity for and support other programming competitions as they come along, such as the Google Code Jam and Global Game Jam.

- Promoting and supporting career events and networking events for students
