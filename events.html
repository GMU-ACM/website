Weekly Meetings

We meet in the Nguyen Engineering Building Room 4201 (ENGR 4201 - CS Conference Room), unless otherwise noted. Meetings typically go until 6 or 7.

If you've never attended a meeting before, please show up to one of our new members meetings. Once you've done that feel free to show up to our other meetings

Competitive Team Meetings are intended for people who want to compete in the next ICPC. You don't have to be competing, but that's the focus of those meetings.
