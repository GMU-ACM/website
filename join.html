Become a member

To join, just show up to one of our meetings, whose times are listed on the Meetings page. New members are always welcome.

Members are highly recommended to:

  • Have taken or are taking CS310, or are at least familiar with data structures like linked lists, trees, and hash tables.
  • Be proficient in programming, whether in C, Python, Java, or whatever language you like.
  • Join our Discord Channel (External link), which is where we announce meeting updates and special events.
