Weekly meetings

PLEASE BRING A LAPTOP! We meet every Thursday from 4:30 pm to 6:30 pm in the Nguyen Engineering Building (ENGR) room 1602.

During the first few meetings we will not focus on any particular data structure or algorithm, and instead solve problems which have a variety of unique solutions. After that we'll focus on problems related to Trees, then Graphs, and finally onto Dynamic Programming (for those new to with dynamic programming, read this guide).

  • Jan 23 – 3sum
  • Jan 30 – Maximum product subarray
  • Feb 6 – First missing positive
  • Feb 13 – Merge k sorted lists Meeting cancelled due to engineers week
  • Feb 20 – Find median from data stream
  • Feb 27 – Top k frequent elements
  • Mar 5 – Interleaving string
  • Mar 19 – Same tree and Invert binary tree
  • Mar 26 – kth smallest element in a binary search tree
  • Apr 2 – Course schedule
  • Apr 9 – Number of islands
  • Apr 16 – House robber
  • Apr 23 – Climbing stairs
  • Apr 30 – Longest increasing subsequence
Haven't come to a meeting before? Feel free to show up at any of our meetings and we'll get you up to speed.

Have a class that ends after the meeting starts? No problem, just come whenever your class ends.
